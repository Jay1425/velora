Our Legacy

Over four decades of tradition, quality, and trust

04 April 1983

On the 4th of April, 1983, in the heart of Kutiyana, Gujarat, a small dream took shape. What began as Rasik Pan & Cold Drinks was more than a business — it was a promise to serve quality, freshness, and authenticity to the local community.

Over the years, our reputation grew not just for refreshing cold drinks and carefully crafted paan, but for something much deeper — the trust we built with every customer, the consistency in every product, and the warmth in every interaction.

As weddings and celebrations became a part of our story, families began requesting something special — premium shrikhand that could honor their most cherished moments. We listened. We experimented. We perfected every flavor with the same dedication that built Rasik Pan & Cold Drinks.

And so, Velora was born — a name that represents elegance, tradition, and celebration. Today, Velora stands as our premium offering, handcrafted for weddings, corporate events, and life's most meaningful occasions.

We don't just make shrikhand. We create experiences. Every kg is a reflection of over 40 years of experience, love for our craft, and commitment to making your celebrations truly memorable.
